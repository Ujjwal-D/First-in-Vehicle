About Us

Discover our story in providing car tinting services.

Our Story

We are First In Vehicles, a family-owned and operated business that has been providing professional car tinting services in Queensland since 2005. With over 18 years of experience, we bring a wealth of knowledge and expertise to every vehicle we service. Our passion for cars and dedication to quality workmanship drive us to use only the best materials and equipment, ensuring that your car windows are tinted to perfection.

Our Mission

At First In Vehicles, our mission is to deliver car tinting services that exceed your expectations. We aim to provide you with the best value for your money, the highest level of customer service, and the most convenient and hassle-free experience. We want you to enjoy the benefits of car tinting, such as enhanced privacy, security, comfort, and aesthetics.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become the leading car tinting service provider in Queensland, known for our expertise, professionalism, and customer satisfaction. We aspire to grow our business by building long-term relationships with our clients and expanding our range of services. We are committed to staying updated with the latest trends and technologies in car tinting.
